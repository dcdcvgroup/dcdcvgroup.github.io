DCD CV Group at Zhejiang University

About Us

We are a computer vision reseach group at Zhejiang University. Our research interest lies in segmentation, lane detection, visual tracking, face recognition, few-shot learning, etc.

Group Leader

Xi Li
Xi Li received the Ph.D. degree from the National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition, Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China, in 2009. From 2009 to 2010, he was a Post-Doctoral Researcher with CNRS Telecom Paris-Tech, France. He was a Senior Researcher with the University of Adelaide, Australia. He is currently a Full Professor with Zhejiang University, China.
